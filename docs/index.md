# EE 5340: Introduction to Quantum Computing and Physical Basics of Computing

Welcome to the intelligent textbook for **EE 5340** at the University of Minnesota.

## About This Course

This course explores how physical principles and limits have been shaping paradigms of computing. A key goal is to understand how a paradigm shift in computing can help with emerging energy challenges.

## Topics Covered

- Physical limits of computing
- Coding and information theoretical foundations
- Computing with beyond-CMOS devices
- Reversible computing
- Quantum computing
- Stochastic computing
