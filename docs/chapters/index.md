# Chapters

This textbook is organized into 16 chapters covering 200 concepts.

## Chapter Overview

1. [Mathematical Foundations](01-mathematical-foundations/index.md) - Core mathematical tools including complex numbers, linear algebra, matrix operations, eigenvalue theory, and probability.
2. [Physical Limits of Computing](02-physical-limits/index.md) - Thermodynamic constraints on computation from Landauer's principle through ultimate speed and information limits.
3. [Information Theory Foundations](03-information-theory/index.md) - Shannon entropy, mutual information, coding theorems, channel capacity, and algorithmic information theory.
4. [Classical Computing Fundamentals](04-classical-computing/index.md) - Boolean algebra, CMOS physics, power dissipation, the Boltzmann tyranny, and computational complexity.
5. [Beyond-CMOS Devices](05-beyond-cmos-devices/index.md) - Emerging device technologies including spintronics, memristors, tunnel FETs, and negative capacitance FETs.
6. [Reversible Computing](06-reversible-computing/index.md) - Logical and physical reversibility, Bennett's proof, Toffoli and Fredkin gates, and adiabatic computing.
7. [Quantum Mechanics Foundations](07-quantum-mechanics/index.md) - Postulates of quantum mechanics, Dirac notation, Hilbert space, measurement, and the Schrodinger equation.
8. [Qubits and Quantum States](08-qubits-and-states/index.md) - Qubit representation, Bloch sphere, entanglement, Bell states, and Schmidt decomposition.
9. [Quantum Gates and Circuits](09-quantum-gates/index.md) - Pauli gates, Hadamard, rotation operators, CNOT, universal gate sets, and the quantum circuit model.
10. [Quantum Algorithms](10-quantum-algorithms/index.md) - Deutsch-Jozsa, QFT, phase estimation, Shor's algorithm, Grover's search, and quantum walks.
11. [Quantum Error Correction](11-quantum-error-correction/index.md) - Noise channels, Kraus operators, stabilizer formalism, surface codes, and fault tolerance.
12. [Quantum Hardware Platforms](12-quantum-hardware/index.md) - Superconducting qubits, trapped ions, photonic qubits, and topological qubits.
13. [Stochastic Computing](13-stochastic-computing/index.md) - Stochastic number encoding, logic gates, variance analysis, and applications.
14. [Quantum-Classical Hybrid Computing](14-hybrid-computing/index.md) - VQE, QAOA, parameterized circuits, barren plateaus, and NISQ-era constraints.
15. [Energy-Efficient Computing Paradigms](15-energy-efficient-computing/index.md) - Neuromorphic, approximate, in-memory, and thermodynamic computing.
16. [Future of Computing](16-future-of-computing/index.md) - Quantum supremacy, quantum advantage, benchmarking, and the post-Moore landscape.
